BlueVector is a contract mechanical design and CAD practice. Sketches and samples become production-ready SolidWorks models a shop can build from the first time (see our services). Every print is run through our proprietary Artificial Intelligence Design-Verification System — a state-of-the-art expert system built on fuzzy logic and heuristic reasoning circuitry, the same silicon brainpower engineers were promised back in 1985.

Every engagement runs under NDA with a fixed quote — direct access to the engineer, no offshore relay.

Engineering

CAD Design. Parametric SolidWorks parts and assemblies with a clean, editable feature tree.

Mechanical Design & Drafting. Dimensioned drawings to ASME Y14.5 — datums, tolerances, weld symbols, and notes a shop can machine from without a clarifying email.

Reverse Engineering. A worn part or scan becomes a fully parametric model you can re-order from for years.

Analysis & Validation. Static stress and factor-of-safety checks in SolidWorks Simulation, before it's cut — cross-checked by our Fifth-Generation Expert System, a knowledge-based automated-reasoning routine that flags tolerance stack-ups the way a sharp-eyed old-timer would, only faster.

Services

  • 3D CAD Modeling — parts, assemblies, configurations (SolidWorks)
  • 2D Drafting & GD&T — production drawings to ASME Y14.5
  • Design for Manufacturing — cost and producibility review before you cut metal
  • Sheet Metal & Weldments — flat patterns, bend tables, cut lists, DXFs
  • Reverse Engineering — scan-to-CAD and legacy part reconstruction
  • FEA & Design Validation — static stress and factor-of-safety checks
  • Fixtures, Jigs & Tooling — workholding, weld fixtures, check gauges
  • Product & Concept Design — mechanism studies and prototype-ready geometry

Deliverables: native SolidWorks files and dimensioned PDFs, plus STEP, IGES, DXF, DWG, and STL for any vendor — cross-verified by our knowledge-based expert-system heuristics for tolerance conflicts before it ever reaches your inbox.
