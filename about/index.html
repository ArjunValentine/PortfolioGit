Controls & Automation

From RFQ to a panel that ships and runs — schematics drawn from scratch, BOMs built, software written, and factory-acceptance-tested before it ever reaches the field.

  • Municipal & industrial pump controls — panel schematics from a blank sheet, BOMs, AutoCAD layouts to UL 508A, PLC/HMI programming, VFD configuration, and factory acceptance testing before field commissioning.
  • Modbus TCP OEE system — a networked data-collection system deployed across a production floor for real-time machine OEE, KPI dashboards, and root-cause data for unplanned downtime, over existing plant infrastructure.
  • Computer-vision & automation integration — OpenCV + depth-camera in-line defect detection and robotic path simulation; wirelessly-controlled pump & filtration arrays for vertical water storage; off-grid remote-worksite design for UAV operations.

Continuous Improvement & Quality Systems

A plant rebuilt around measurement — lean events, corrective-action systems, and ISO 9001:2015 readiness, scoped across production, blending, shipping, fab shop, and stocking.

  • 8D / DMAIC CAPA system — designed and implemented a closed-loop corrective-and-preventive-action procedure; every nonconformance travels Define → Measure → Analyze → Improve → Control, with zero recurrence on closed corrective-action reports.
  • RotoLoad automated resin dispensing — integrated a gravimetric weigh-and-dispense system into an existing powder network, cutting resin-handling labor 37% and eliminating manual weigh-out and ladder charging entirely.
  • 5S / Kaizen rapid-improvement events — 24-hour events across multiple work areas: new work centers fabricated, clutter removed, standardized tool stations and floor lanes.
  • Systems & standardization — machine processing parameters standardized by key variable, ERP BOM verification against machine usage, value-stream mapping, and OSHA-compliant chemical storage & labeling.

Manufacturing & Mechanical Engineering

Where the parts and tooling come from.

  • Tooling & production engineering — quoting, designing, and releasing tooling and production plans in SolidEdge and SolidWorks across rotational molding, injection molding, and 3D-printing processes, with GD&T-compliant drafting and weldment detailing.
  • QMS stewardship — progressed through design, quality, and QA/QC roles on the way to owning an ISO 9001:2015 quality management system.
  • Rapid prototyping — designed and built a custom FFF 3D printer from scratch, cutting departmental prototyping cost 50%, and produced printing filament on-site for material independence.
