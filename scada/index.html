How this reads live data
Live stage comes from USGS real-time gauges inside the Winter Haven bounding box (served through a Netlify function). Each segment's clearance is its seeded channel depth (from DEM/LiDAR — the datum no API hands you) minus how far the real lake has drawn down below its recent full pool. Datum-agnostic on purpose. The slider scrubs the observed record — every point a measurement, none simulated.
The honest part
This informs, it does not guarantee navigability. Channel-bottom depths are seeded estimates pending survey. A conditions estimate — never a promise there is water under your hull.
